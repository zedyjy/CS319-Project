CS299
Summer Training I Description:

Description: Conducted in a company setting with involvement in real projects for a minimum of four weeks (20 working days). Application of knowledge and skills learned at school to solve engineering problems related to computer systems in the real-world. Familiarization with professional and ethical responsibility while working in multidisciplinary teams. Understanding the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and societal context. Learning to find relevant resources to access information. Observation of the use of contemporary tools, techniques, standards and methods. Preparing technical documentation. Credit units: None. ECTS Credit units: 6, Prerequisite: CS 202.
